SPACKLE HELPS YOU AVOID THESE COMMON MISTAKES

BAD LAYOUT 
Function dictates design. The right 2D & 3D plans will prevent awkward spacing in any part of your bathroom. 
UNREALISTIC BUDGET 
Know what to expect. We prepare you with materials lists, labor & time estimates, etc. so you avoid costly surprises. 
INSUFFICIENT STORAGE 
Don’t make storage an afterthought. You always need more space than you think for towels and toiletries. 
INACCURATE MEASUREMENTS 
Faulty measurements will derail an entire project. We help you get the correct ones so you can plan and build accordingly. 
INCORRECT MATERIALS 
From waterproofing to smart fixtures & finishes, we ensure you get the right materials and deliver them to your door. 
POOR VENTILATION 
Mold grows where water lingers. Equip your space with proper ventilation to withstand changes in moisture and temperature. 
 

COMMON QUESTIONS

  • What’s the cost of bathroom renovation with Spackle?

    Spackle doesn’t renovate bathrooms. Instead, we make it easy for you to do some or all of the work yourself.

    Our Estimator calculates what your renovation might cost if you did parts of it yourself or hired a contractor, as well as the time and effort involved. You decide what you want to DIY and what you’d prefer to hire out.

    We facilitate a stress-free, high-quality remodel on your terms. By empowering you to do more of the work yourself, you control how much your new bathroom will cost.

  • I have zero construction experience. Can Spackle really help me renovate my bathroom myself?

    Yes, that’s why we’re here! We tailor your design plan to your abilities, then send you all the right tools and materials, step-by-step instructions, and videos to help you execute every stage successfully. And with the support of a dedicated project coordinator and on-demand remote assistance from a Pro, you’re never on your own. Whether you want to swing a sledgehammer or simply manage the project and let someone else do the heavy lifting, you can be a Spackler.

  • Absolutely! You don’t need to do any construction if you don’t want to. You can use us for the design and project management tools and hire out all or most of the work. We’ll still be by your side to make sure everything is done right and looks great when it’s complete.

  • Yes, you can. Because there are so many fixtures, finishes, and materials you need for your renovation, we designed Spackle to streamline the decision-making process and source quality products that are also DIY-friendly. But we always strive to help you, no matter your chosen products.

  • Our online design service includes meetings and unlimited messaging with a dedicated designer. After a kick-off to discuss your bathroom renovation, your designer will help you select the fixtures & finishes you need to bring your vision to life. You’ll receive a complete list of products ready for you to shop on the Spackle platform, with the ability to swap out stuff as often as you like until you’re 100% satisfied.

    In the final stage, you’ll receive a 3D rendering of your new bathroom and detailed 2D construction plans of your new layout adhering to National Kitchen and Bath Association standards, with up to two additional revisions to ensure the design plans are exactly what you envision.

  • Before creating your design plans, we divide your renovation into stages. To each, we attach estimates of time and effort; you choose which stages you want to DIY or hire out.

    Once we know what you’re doing yourself, we adjust the design plan to your skill level. That way, you don’t end up with the kind of tile that makes even professionals tremble. Trust us, we’ve been there. (Live and learn.)

    We strive to create the most precise plans for your bathroom, but you or your contractor may need to modify them during construction. Sometimes, new obstacles (and opportunities) present themselves when you begin to demo.
